Staff

The staff of the Bryan Symphony Orchestra is a small but mighty team working tirelessly behind the scenes to ensure every performance, event, and initiative runs smoothly. Each member brings unique expertise—from operations and development to marketing and production—and it’s their collaboration, clear communication, and shared commitment to excellence that keeps the BSO thriving. Their dedication and teamwork are the unseen rhythm behind every note you hear.
